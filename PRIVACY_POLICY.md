# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

Chess Champ ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## 1. Introduction
Chess Champ respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Profile Information:
- Display name
- Email address (optional)
- Country
- Gender preference
- Profile picture

### Gameplay Data:
- Match history and statistics
- Level and rank progression
- Virtual coin transactions
- Puzzle completion data

### Device Information:
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Crash reports and performance data

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- Provide and improve game services
- Personalize your gaming experience
- Process transactions and subscriptions
- Send important notifications
- Detect and prevent cheating or fraud
- Respond to customer support requests

## 4. Data Storage
Your game data is stored locally on your device and may be backed up to secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data during transmission and storage.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:
- Service providers (hosting, analytics)
- Legal authorities when required by law
- Other players (public profile information only)

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt out of marketing communications

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking
We use local storage and similar technologies to save your preferences and game progress. This data remains on your device unless you choose to sync or backup.

## 9. Third-Party Services
The App may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services.

## 10. Updates to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.
